The full, current OFL 1.1 FAQ is maintained by SIL and is available at:

    https://openfontlicense.org
    https://silnrsi.github.io/silfontdev/ (Font Development Best Practices)

The Beeraw Hex fonts are released under the SIL Open Font License, Version
1.1, with the Reserved Font Name "Beeraw Hex" (see OFL.txt).

Short summary of what the OFL allows and requires:

  - You MAY use, study, embed, modify and redistribute the fonts freely,
    including in commercial products and documents.
  - You MAY bundle and sell the fonts WITH other software.
  - You MAY NOT sell the fonts on their own.
  - You MAY NOT release a modified version under the Reserved Font Name
    "Beeraw Hex" — rename any derivative.
  - Modified versions MUST remain under the OFL and MUST keep the copyright
    notice and license.
  - Documents created WITH the fonts are NOT restricted by the license.

For the authoritative and complete FAQ, always refer to the SIL site above.
